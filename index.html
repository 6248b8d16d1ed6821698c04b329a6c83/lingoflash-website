Features

Everything you need to master a language

Built around how memory actually works — with real phrases, native audio, and visible progress.

🔄

Flip cards

Tap to reveal — see the phrase in your language, flip to see the translation. Simple, effective, addictive.

🔊

Native pronunciation

Every card reads aloud in the correct accent — French with a French accent, Arabic with an Arabic accent.

📈

Track your progress

See exactly how many phrases you've completed per topic, with a daily streak to keep you coming back.

🗂️

Organised by topic

From transport to greetings to food — browse by category and focus on what matters to you right now.

✍️

Latin transcription

Arabic and Russian cards show both the local script and a Latin transliteration — so you can read it out loud immediately.

📱

Works offline

All content is stored on your device. Study on the subway, on a plane — no internet needed, ever.

How LingoFlash works

Three simple steps to real progress.

1

Pick your languages

Choose the language you speak and the one you want to learn. Switch any time from the settings.

2

Browse by topic

Pick a category — transport, greetings, food — and dive into real phrases grouped by theme.

3

Flip, listen, complete

Tap to flip each card, listen to the pronunciation, and mark it done. Watch your progress fill up.
